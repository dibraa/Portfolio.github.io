ACTIVITIES


Technology Timeline Infographics

September 2, 2024


From this activity, I learned how significant the Transistor Era (1947-1962) was in shaping modern technology. The invention of the transistor by John Bardeen, Walter Brattain, and William Shockley replaced bulky vacuum tubes, leading to smaller and more efficient devices like early computers and portable radios. I understood how this innovation not only revolutionized industries like computing and telecommunications but also impacted society by making technology more accessible. This activity taught me the importance of technological advancements and how collaboration and innovation can create lasting changes that continue to influence our lives today.

Cybercrime Case Activity

September 15, 2024


From this activity, I learned about the serious consequences of identity theft and how it can affect individuals and communities. The case of Mark Joseph Lontok, a public school teacher in the Philippines, showed how sharing personal information online, like posting a photo of his PRC ID, led to significant financial and emotional difficulties. This incident highlights the importance of protecting sensitive data and being cautious about online behavior. I also realized the need for stricter regulations from institutions like banks and government agencies to prevent such crimes, as well as public awareness campaigns to educate people on the dangers of oversharing personal details. Overall, this activity taught me how crucial it is to safeguard personal information in the digital age.

HTML Activity 1

October 23, 2024


From this HTML activity, I learned how to structure a basic webpage using HTML elements effectively. The document showcases the use of various tags like html, head, and body to define the structure of a webpage. The h1 to h6 tags were used to create headings of different sizes, demonstrating how to organize content hierarchically. Lists were created using ul for unordered lists and li for list items, which are useful for presenting data like hobbies and pet peeves in a clear and organized way.

Wireframe Creation for Your Portfolio Website

October 29, 2024


From this activity, I learned the importance of creating a wireframe as a foundational step in website development. A wireframe helps visualize the layout and structure of a webpage by organizing content, navigation, and user interface elements in a simple, design-neutral format. This process allows me to plan the flow of information and functionality of the site effectively before writing any code. I also realized how wireframes make it easier to communicate ideas and align them with project goals, ensuring a clear vision for the final design. This activity taught me that planning ahead through wireframing can save time, reduce errors, and result in a more user-friendly website.
